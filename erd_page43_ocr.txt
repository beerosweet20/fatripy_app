6.6 Entity Relationship Diagram

Diagram Description:

This Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) represents the complete database struc-
ture of the Fatripy application. It shows the main entities (tables), their attributes
(fields), and the relationships between them. The diagram includes eight primary
entities: User, FamilyGroup, GroupMember, TripPlan, Accommodation, Activity,
TripPlan_Activity, and Booking. Each entity contains specific attributes with pri-
mary keys for identification and foreign keys to establish connections between related
entities. The relationships are defined with cardinality indicators (1:1, 1:M, M:M)
showing how entities interact with each other. This structure supports the core
functionality of generating personalized trip plans, managing family information, or-
ganizing activities and accommodations, and handling booking processes. The design
ensures data integrity and enables efficient retrieval of information needed to create

comprehensive travel plans tailored to family needs and preferences.
