Preface
Smart applications have become an integral part of contemporary life, offering tools
that save time, reduce effort, and simplify everyday tasks. One of the persistent
challenges faced by families is trip planning, which involves identifying suitable des-
tinations, activities, accommodations, and dining options that cater to diverse age
groups and preferences. This process is often time-consuming and complex.
Toaddressthischallenge, theFatripyprojectwasdevelopedasanintelligentassistant
designed specifically for families traveling within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The
application generates a comprehensive daily plan that includes a variety of activities,
tourist attractions, restaurants, and lodging options. It also features direct booking
capabilities and a “Book Now, Pay on Arrival” option. Through these features,
Fatripy aims to enhance the family travel experience by making it more enjoyable,
organized, and effortless.

ABSTRACT
This project presents an intelligent assistant designed to support the planning of
family trips within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The proposed mobile application
gathers essential input from users, including the number of family members, their
age groups, trip duration, and the allocated budget. Based on these inputs, the
system performs data analysis to generate a comprehensive travel plan. The plan
incorporates tailored recommendations for family-oriented tourist attractions, age-
appropriate activities, dining options, and accommodation choices consistent with
the specified budget. In addition, the system provides a structured daily plan and
integrates booking functionalities for selected services and activities. The primary
objective of the project is to streamline the process of trip planning by minimizing
the time and effort required for information searching, while simultaneously offering
a personalized and engaging travel experience for all family members.
KEYWORDS
Trip planning, family travel, intelligent system, digital tourism guide, activity recom-
mendations, budget management, travel plan, booking integration, tourism in Saudi
Arabia, mobile application, personalized travel experience.

1.1 Project Overview Statement
The tourism sector in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia is experiencing rapid growth in
alignment with Vision 2030, which emphasizes cultural, economic, and recreational
development. Families represent a key segment of the domestic tourism market;
however, planning family trips remains a complex process due to several challenges:
•Diverse family needs Preferences vary significantly between children, teenagers,
and adults, making it difficult to choose a single plan that satisfies all age
groups.
•Budget limitations Families must balance enjoyment with financial constraints,
requiring careful decisions regarding accommodations, dining, and activities.
•Time-consuming planning Gathering accurate and up-to-date information
about tourist destinations, events, and lodging options can be overwhelming.
•Lack of choice and flexibility Most digital tools offer a single, automated plan,
leaving families with no alternatives to compare, which can feel restrictive.
To address these issues, Fatripy functions as a personalized family travel assistant
that simplifies trip planning by generating multiple, automatically created, ready-
made plans based on simple user input:
--- Page 13 ---
2
•Number of family members.
•Age groups (children, adults, seniors).
•Trip duration.
•Budget.
•Desired city or region to visit.
Based on this input, the app creates three distinct, pre-designed plan options tailored
to the family’s profile. The user can then fully review and compare these plans before
making a final choice. Each complete plan includes:
•A suggested hotel with its total cost for the entire trip duration.
•Recommendations for nearby activities close to the hotel (restaurants, cafes,
parks, beaches).
•Recommendations for distant attractions and key landmarks to visit during
the day.
•A pre-structured daily schedule that integrates all elements.
What sets Fatripy apart from traditional tourism apps is its ability to automatically
generatemultiplefulltravelschedules,givingtheuserthepowerofchoice,ratherthan
forcing them to accept a single option or build one manually. This automation and
flexibility significantly reduce the time and effort families usually invest in planning,
while encouraging domestic tourism across Saudi Arabia.

tourism market, a specific gap exists for a dedicated, all-in-one family trip planning
tool that empowers the user with choice.Existing apps are either focused on religious
tourism, act as informational hubs without integrated booking, or lack practical
planning features like budget management and comparative plan building.

multiple, ready-to-use travel itineraries from which the user can choose. This core
feature, combined with budget management and direct booking capabilities within a
single, user-friendly application, effectively addresses a clear and underserved market
segment.
